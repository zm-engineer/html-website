About

The Traveler Behind the Site

Hi, I'm Ziuling Macayo Lopez — a web developer with 8 years of experience who discovered a passion for travel four years ago and hasn't been able to stop since. What started as a curiosity became my favorite way to recharge: when I have a free weekend, I grab the car and head out to villages or nearby cities; when I have more time, I cross borders. I'm not a luxury traveler or an extreme adventurer.

I'm the person who gets lost on purpose, eats at the stall with the longest local queue, and spends an entire afternoon watching a harbor wake up. My most recent adventures took me through Japan and South Korea — an experience that reminded me why I keep moving. Next stop: Australia, where I'm heading in a month to study English and, of course, explore everything I can. Dream Destinations is where I keep notes on the places that moved me most, written for friends who keep asking, "So where should I go next?"

Everything here comes from real curiosity rather than sponsorship. If a place made it onto this site, it's because I genuinely think it's worth the trip.
